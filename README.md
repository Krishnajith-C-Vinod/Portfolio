# Krishnajith C Vinod — Research Portfolio

A futuristic quantum-inspired personal portfolio website built to showcase my academic background, research interests, publications, and ongoing work in quantum information science and emerging computational paradigms.

## About

I am a Physics researcher from Kozhikode, Kerala, India. I completed my BSc in Physics from Zamorins Guruvayurappan College and my MSc in Physics from Government Arts and Science College Meenchanda, graduating as the First Rank Holder under the University of Calicut in 2025. I am currently pursuing my PhD in Physics at IIT Madras.

My research interests include:

- Quantum Information Science
- Quantum Machine Learning
- Quantum Neural Networks
- Quantum Teleportation
- Emergent Computation
- Condensed Matter Physics
